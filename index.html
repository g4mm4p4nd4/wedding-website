How we met

A true testament of the power of "swiping right," we met on a dating app called Hinged. I scrolled through David's profile and almost ignored it until I stumbled on a picture of him standing next to a wookie. Mildly interested I sent him a message, "I know you're tall, but that was a very small wookie." From there, David's use of complete sentences, proper grammar, and level of nerdiness intrigued me enough to meet him in person. On New Year's Day we met at Starbucks. Of course we both contemplated backing out, but David felt bad and I was playing a Starbucks game and would get extra points for going that day. I saw him from across the parking lot and thought, "he's not 6' 3" and almost got back in the car. But I didn't. Soon I would be surprised. Not only was he actually 6' 3", he was also curious, confident, and had the best posture of anyone I had seen since band. We sat there for three hours talking, only stopping because it was cold and I had to go to the bathroom. Looking back, I see all the ways we could have missed each other. I am just so grateful two people took a couple of wild chances and found their way to one another.
